Who are we?

an AI-powered location scouting platform for media production companies. We leverage advanced technologies to provide a seamless user experience using location scouting process With VR Technology and empowering our customers to focus on creative storytelling

With Stepin Locations, you can find and visit shooting locations faster and easier than ever. Our AI-powered platform searches through a massive database of locations, taking into account your specific criteria, such as budget, location, and type of shoot.

How to schedule ?

Once you've found a few potential locations, you can use our platform to schedule visits and get in touch with location owners. We also offer a variety of other features, such as location scouting reports and 3D previews, to help you make the best decision for your production.

Our team has to plan every single step of the project to make sure that the final result will meet your requirements, so we do our best to schedule a visti in the perfect day/night time for best scenery.

What we aim for ?

to create a platform that makes movie locations accessible to everyone saving money and efforts of choosing locations, powered with the latest technologies of AR/VR and AI search mechanisms we tail an experience just to your taste.

For that we gathered collections of locations from all over the world mixed with AI and new gen tech to be in the center of the action, leaving your most valuable assets in your hands, saving money and giving a brand new experience to the cinema industry.
